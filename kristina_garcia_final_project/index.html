What's Wrong?

Panic Attack Here's the symptoms

Fear of dying, losing control, or going crazy.

Numbness or tingling sensations, chest pain or discomfort.

Trembling or shaking, shortness of breath.

Hot or cold flashes,choking feeling.

Feeling Dizzy, Racing heart, Sweating, Nausea.

Anxiety Here's the symptoms

Racing or unwanted thoughts.

Fatigue, restlessness, or sweating.

Hypervigilance or irritability.

Fear, nausea, sensation of an abnormal heartbeat, or trembling.

Depression Here's the symptoms

Apathy, boredom, general discontent, hopelessness, inability to feel pleasure, loneliness, loss of interest, mood swings, sadness, poor appetite, self harm, or emotional distress.

Stress Here's the symptoms

Low energy, headaches, upset stomach.

Aches, pains, and tense muscles, chest pain and rapid heartbeat.

Insomnia, frequent colds and infections

Nervousness and shaking

Cold or sweaty hands and feet, excess sweating

Clenched jaw and grinding teeth.

Insomnia Here's the symptoms

Lack of concentration or slowness in activity and thought

Sleeping difficulty or daytime sleepiness

Also common: depression, headache, or irritability
